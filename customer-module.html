Customer Management Overview

The Customer Module allows managing customer records for orders and communication.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, view, and delete customers
  • Assign customer orders
  • Export and import customer lists
  • Store contact and billing details

Viewing Customers

  1. Navigate to Customers from the menu
  2. Browse all registered customers
  3. Search and filter by name or contact


Creating Customers

  1. Click Create Customer
  2. Fill in:
    • Name, Email, Phone Number
    • Address and City
    • Customer Category
  3. Click Save
Note: Phone and email must be unique.


Editing Customers

  1. Choose the customer to modify
  2. Click Edit
  3. Update fields and Save


Deleting Customers

  1. Open the customer details
  2. Click Delete
  3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog
Warning: Deleted customers cannot be recovered.


Exporting Customers

  1. Click Export in the customer list
  2. Download the Excel file


Importing Customers

  1. Click Import
  2. Upload a valid Excel file
  3. Click Import to confirm
Note: Download sample Excel format before importing.
